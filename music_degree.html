About Our School

The A.R. Rahman Center for Musical Excellence is dedicated to nurturing musical talent through a blend of traditional and contemporary education. Founded with the vision of our patron, Academy Award-winning composer A.R. Rahman, our institution provides world-class music education to aspiring musicians.

Our curriculum is designed to provide a solid foundation in music theory, history, and performance, while also encouraging innovation and creative expression.

A.R. Rahman

Featured Instrument: Piano

The piano has been instrumental in A.R. Rahman's musical journey and compositions. As a versatile instrument that can produce both melody and harmony, the piano serves as the foundation for many of his iconic works.

Why Study Piano

  • Develop strong fundamental music skills
  • Master harmony, rhythm, and musical expression
  • Prepare for careers in performance, composition, and accompaniment
  • Enhance cognitive abilities and discipline

Our Piano Program

Our comprehensive piano curriculum includes classical training, jazz improvisation, film score interpretation, and contemporary techniques. Students work with master pianists and have access to our state-of-the-art piano labs and practice rooms.
