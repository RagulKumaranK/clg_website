Our Distinguished Faculty

Dr. Anand Kumar

Department Head, Vocal Music

Hindustani Classical Music Theory

With over 25 years of experience in classical music, Dr. Kumar has performed at prestigious venues across India and abroad. He holds a PhD in Music from Delhi University.

PhD in Music, Delhi University
Sangeet Natak Akademi Award

Prof. Lakshmi Menon

Professor, Instrumental Music

Carnatic Veena Composition

Prof. Menon is a renowned Veena player with expertise in Carnatic classical traditions. She has composed music for various national cultural events and has numerous albums to her credit.

Kerala State Music Academy Award
15+ Music Albums

Dr. Rajiv Sharma

Associate Professor, Music Technology

Sound Engineering Digital Composition Film Scoring

Dr. Sharma bridges traditional music with modern technology. He has worked with leading music directors in the film industry and specializes in digital music production and sound engineering.

Sound Engineer for 30+ Films
MSc in Audio Technology, UK

Spark of Greatness

A.R. Rahman

Music Composer & Singer

Piano Synthesizer Vocals

Academy Award-winning composer known as the "Mozart of Madras". A pioneer in combining Indian classical music with electronic music, world music genres and traditional orchestral arrangements.

2 Academy Awards
6 National Film Awards

Ilaiyaraaja

Music Composer & Conductor

Orchestra Classical Film Music

Widely regarded as one of the greatest Indian music composers. Known for his integration of Indian folk music and classical ragas with Western classical music arrangements.

5 National Film Awards
Padma Vibhushan Award

Late M.S. Subbulakshmi

Carnatic Vocalist

Carnatic Classical Devotional

The first musician ever to be awarded the Bharat Ratna. Known as the "Queen of Music", she was the first Indian musician to receive the Ramon Magsaysay award.

Bharat Ratna
Ramon Magsaysay Award
