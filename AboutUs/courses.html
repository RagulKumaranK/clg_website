Bachelor of Performing Arts

The Bachelor of Performing Arts is a vibrant Programme crafted for students passionate about Classical and Contemporary Performance Arts. It offers specialized training in Dance, Music, and Theatre, Blending Rigorous Practice with Theoretical Knowledge of Aesthetics, Performance History, and Stagecraft.

B. Sc. Indology

B. Sc. Indology is a unique Interdisciplinary Course that delves deep into the study of Indian Civilization, Languages, Literature, Philosophy, Art, and Culture. It offers a comprehensive understanding of India's Ancient Texts, Archaeological Heritage, Spiritual Traditions, and Socio-Political Evolution.

B. Sc. Radiology and Imaging Technology

B. Sc. Radiology and Imaging Technology is a Dynamic Programme designed to train students in Diagnostic Imaging Techniques such as X-ray, CT, MRI, and Ultrasound. The course blends Anatomy, Pathology, and Physics with practical instruction in using Advanced Radiological Equipment. Students are trained to assist Radiologists in Diagnosis and Ensure Safe, Accurate Imaging Procedures.

B. Sc. Medical Laboratory Technology

B.Sc. in Medical Laboratory Technology (MLT) Programme equips students with the knowledge and skills to conduct Clinical Laboratory Tests essential for Disease Detection, Diagnosis, and Treatment. Covering Biochemistry, Microbiology, Hematology, and Pathology, the Curriculum emphasizes Accuracy, Quality Control, and Lab Safety. Students receive rigorous training in Operating Diagnostic Equipment and Interpreting Laboratory Results.

B. Tech. Aviation & Drone Engineering

B. Tech. in Aviation & Drone Engineering is a specialized course that prepares students for careers in the fast-growing Aerospace and UAV (Unmanned Aerial Vehicle) Sectors. The Programme covers Aircraft Design, Flight Mechanics, Avionics, and Drone Technology. With a curriculum grounded in Aeronautics and Emerging Drone Systems, students gain experience in Simulation Labs, Drone Assembly and Flight Training.

B. Tech. Artificial Intelligence & Medical Imaging

B. Tech. Programme combines the power of Artificial Intelligence with Medical Imaging Technologies to reshape Healthcare Diagnostics. Students learn AI Fundamentals, Deep Learning, Image Processing and Data Analytics, with applications in Radiology, Pathology and Smart Medical Systems. The course includes Lab-Based Learning, Simulations, and Industry-Aligned Projects that emphasize Precision Medicine and Automation in Healthcare.
